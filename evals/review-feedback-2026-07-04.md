# Review Feedback Cases - 2026-07-04

This file captures the second review pass on the 10 fresh cases. The goal is to preserve the user's preferred humanized style and turn the feedback into reusable rules.

## Forbidden Structures

Do not open with a negative contrast before the positive claim.

Avoid quoted framing labels as the main explanation.

Avoid colon-led explanation sentences when a direct sentence works.

Avoid `X + Y + Z` shorthand as the core sentence structure. Use a normal subject, verb, and object.

Avoid habit-breaking negative titles in PPT, such as telling the reader what not to do before saying the useful action.

Use negative contrast only when the rejected object is specific and necessary.

## Replacement Structures

| Avoid | Prefer |
|---|---|
| Negative setup, then positive point | Positive point first, with the concrete object and action |
| Quoted label as metaphor | Plain noun phrase or direct claim |
| `claim: explanation` | Full sentence, or `metric—meaning` when it is truly a metric |
| Three abstract nouns joined as shorthand | A concrete subject doing one clear action |
| PPT title built around stopping a habit | PPT title built around the action to take |

## Case 1 - Chinese Weekly Update

**Original AI-ish output**

本周我们围绕核心目标持续推进产品迭代工作，通过跨部门协同和高效沟通，进一步提升了项目交付效率。目前搜索模块已完成联调，推荐模块仍需算法确认口径，埋点数据存在延迟问题。下一步我们将持续优化流程，确保项目高质量交付。

**Humanized output**

本周主要我们推进了三块：搜索模块已经联调完，推荐模块的口径还要等算法确认，埋点数据现在有点延迟。比较影响交付的是第二项，如果算法明天还没给最终口径，前端这边可能就要先按旧版本走。我今天会把待确认项列出来发给算法和数据，明天下午一起同步结果。

**What changed**

Kept natural word order, time adverbs, and a concrete next action.

## Case 2 - Chinese Social Post

**Original AI-ish output**

真正的成长不是一味追求效率，而是学会与自己长期相处。每一个微小的坚持，都会在时间的复利下，成为更好的自己。

**Humanized output**

最近越来越觉得，成长这件事不用每天都搞得很燃。比如我以前特别爱列计划，一页纸写满，第二天就开始拖。现在呢我只留两个小动作：睡前收一下桌子，早上先喝水再看手机。听起来很小，但能反复做到，心里会稳一点。总之先别急着变厉害，能坚持下来才比较重要。

**What changed**

Kept a light oral rhythm and concrete daily details.

## Case 3 - Customer Reply

**Original AI-ish output**

非常抱歉给您带来了不便。我们已高度重视该问题，并将立即协调相关团队进行排查和优化，后续也会持续完善服务流程，感谢您的理解与支持。

**Humanized output**

抱歉，这次确实让您多等了。我们查了一下，问题出在库存状态没有及时同步，客服这边看到的信息慢了一步。今天 17:00 前我会给您一个明确处理结果；如果需要换货，我们这边直接帮您走加急。这次多出来的等待时间我们会来承担，以免您再反复确认。

**What changed**

Named the issue, deadline, and resolution without empty apology language.

## Case 4 - Chinese Investment Analysis

**Original AI-ish output**

该公司未来有望受益于行业需求增长和国产替代趋势，但仍需关注订单兑现、毛利率变化、客户集中度和竞争格局等风险。

**Humanized output**

单看国产替代这个逻辑现在可能不太够了，要看看新签的这些订单能不能从验证单进入批量交付，以及放量以后毛利率能不能守住。如果后续公告还是停留在合作框架，没有交付节奏和收入确认，那么估值上就很难继续给溢价。风险也还要看两个点：大客户议价会不会压毛利，以及替代供应商什么时候进来。

**What changed**

Used a softer investment-research transition and kept the risk objects concrete.

## Case 5 - English Founder Update

**Original AI-ish output**

We are excited to share that the team has made meaningful progress across product, customer development, and go-to-market execution. We completed 18 customer interviews, launched the first workflow prototype, and will continue to build scalable foundations for long-term growth.

**Humanized output**

We completed 18 customer interviews and got the first workflow prototype in front of users. Next step we should focus on whether teams come back to the workflow after the first try, or is it still just interesting in a demo. That is what we should learn before adding more GTM motion.

**What changed**

Removed quoted framing and colon-led question setup. The output states the concrete next step directly.

## Case 6 - English Follow-Up Email

**Original AI-ish output**

Dear Michael, I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to kindly follow up on the proposal we shared last Friday and would appreciate any updates you may be able to provide at your earliest convenience.

**Humanized output**

Hi Michael,

Just following up on the proposal from last Friday. Even if the timeline is still moving, do you have a rough sense of whether this is still active on your side? It would help us decide whether to keep the implementation slot open for next week.

Best,

**What changed**

Removed default politeness and kept the practical reason for asking.

## Case 9 - PPT Page

**Original AI-ish output**

标题：AI 提效方案

- 背景：企业效率提升需求显著增加
- 痛点：流程复杂、协同低效、数据分散
- 方案：通过 AI 工具实现流程自动化
- 价值：提升效率、降低成本、促进增长

**Humanized output**

**寻找进度卡壳点**

- 当前卡点集中在审批来回确认、文件版本查找和状态更新维护。
- 构建持续重复工作流程，以审批、文件版本和状态更新为第一组观察对象。
- 30天重点关注指标—交接消耗时间。
- 该指标应是重点优先关注对象，或许比一昧接入AI工具更能提升效率。

**What changed**

Removed the habit-breaking negative title, avoided decorative labels, and used a metric dash instead of colon-led explanation.

## Case 10 - Image Prompt

**Original AI-ish output**

A futuristic AI productivity workspace, ultra-detailed, cinematic lighting, 8K, masterpiece, sleek glassmorphism dashboard, floating cards, purple gradient, modern office, highly realistic.

**Humanized output**

Hero image for an AI productivity tool landing page, wide 16:9 crop. Keep the left third quiet for headline text added later. On the right, show a real desk setup with a laptop, a notebook with messy task notes, and one monitor with dashboard-like chart shapes and table blocks. No readable UI text.

Late afternoon window light, neutral wall, warm gray desk surface, muted blue and green accents on the screen. Keep it practical and slightly lived-in. Avoid floating UI cards, purple gradient, glassmorphism, readable text, and overdone cinematic lighting.

**What changed**

Replaced quality tags with concrete composition, use case, lighting, and exclusions.
